Revised Rules

  • In each section a selected character will give X points if they die, or subtract X points if they survive.

  • If they are unconfirmed (looks like they died but it's not certain, i.e. off screen) there will be no gain or loss.

  • If they die, and you left them in the "survive" column, you lose 1 point.

  • If they weren't in the episode and you picked them to die, you don't lose points
