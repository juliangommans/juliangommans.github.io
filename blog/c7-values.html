Exploring your values

In general I like to live up to the following values (out of 5):

  • Accountability - 3
  • Compassion - 5
  • Creativity - 4
  • Honesty - 5
  • Introspection - 4
  • Fairness - 5
  • Justice - 5
  • Privacy - 4
  • Efficiency - 4
  • Freedom - 5
  • Independence - 4
  • Insight - 4

I find that fairness is a very important value that I strive to maintain in my life. Everyone wants to have a fair deal and there are still a lot of people in the world who don't get theirs. I try my hardest to be as fair as possible and be understanding/include as many people as possible.

I feel happy and like I can still improve when I look at my values. I don't really suffer from a lot of Stereotype threat so I'm not overly concerned. I think I could do with going for a walk when I'm feeling down or overwhelemed while on-site. My main concern is getting stuck in a "rabbit hole", but with regular breaks for coffee, a chat and/or a walk I think it should be managable.
